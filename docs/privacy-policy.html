Privacy Policy  ·  Last updated: May 10, 2025

Your privacy matters to us. Yumegami is designed to respect your data — we collect as little as possible and keep everything transparent.
No account required

1 Information We Collect

Yumegami does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not require account registration. No analytics tracking or personally identifiable information is ever sent to our servers.

2 Local Storage

The app stores your favorite wallpapers locally on your device using Android Shared Preferences. This data never leaves your device and is automatically removed when you uninstall the app.

3 Permissions Used

  • Internet — required to load wallpaper images from our public CDN.
  • Network State — used to detect offline status and show an appropriate message.
  • Storage / Photos — accessed only when you choose to save a wallpaper to your gallery.
  • Set Wallpaper — used only when you tap "Set as Wallpaper".

4 Third-Party Services

Yumegami displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and ad-interaction data according to Google's own privacy policy. You can review it here: policies.google.com/privacy


Wallpaper images are delivered from GitHub via the jsDelivr CDN. No personal data is shared with these providers beyond what any standard web request sends (i.e., your IP address).

5 Children's Privacy

Yumegami is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including minors.

6 Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
